### [Left Column / Core Concept 1]
**The National Master Repository**

*   **Single Source of Truth:** Upgrades to the Master Template seamlessly cascade down to all active regions.

### [Right Column / Core Concept 2]
**The Local "Delta-Shadow" Matrix**
*   **Precision Overrides:** Regional facilities do not clone the database. They simply store a lightweight "Shadow" that only tracks their specific edits (e.g., local sponsors or custom shop duration).
*   **Zero Desyncs:** Local customizations never overwrite the National Master, ensuring strict compliance across all states.

### [Bottom Bar / Technical Outcome]
**Real-Time Resolution Engine**
When a student logs in, the engine instantly merges the National Baseline with their specific Regional Overrides into a single, highly-performant data payload—guaranteeing they see their local reality without degrading processing speed.
